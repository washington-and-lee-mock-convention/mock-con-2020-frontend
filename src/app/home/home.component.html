How often data is updated

We do our best to update the site when new components or applications exit the beta phase. We only deploy the final product of a project. So if there is something you'd like us to direct our attention to, then feel free to reach out and we'll do our best to distribute our resources accordingly.

As of now, the dev team is working on two major projects. One is the construction of profiles for each candidate. The other is a map/library that will attempt display the voting histories of different voting districts, their demographics, and any relevant trends in population.

We will try our best to cite our sources on the data we collect. If you would like to investigate something further and do not see a link to the appropriate resource, then please let us know.
