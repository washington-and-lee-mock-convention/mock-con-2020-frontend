About the project...

The purpose of this site is to provide the analysts of the 27th Mock Convention of Washington and Lee University a repository of information concerning the Democratic Nominee for the 2020 Election. This is the first time a Mock Convention has had a branch devoted to collecting the relevant data. Seeing as demographic and election data is being publisehd online, it would be novice to bypass such a vast resource of information. Additionally, with the grand number of incubments competing for the nomination, having a site that maintains an up-to-date and accurate pool of data is invaluable.
